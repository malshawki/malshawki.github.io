About

I study the computational principles of learning and memory in the brain, with a focus on neural circuit dynamics. My lab combines modeling, theory, and collaboration with experimentalists to understand how the brain represents information over time.

Research

  • Neural Dynamics of Memory: Modeling recurrent networks and synaptic plasticity.
  • Neuromorphic Systems: Translating biological principles to machine intelligence.
  • Interdisciplinary Collaborations: Joint work with cognitive scientists and roboticists.
